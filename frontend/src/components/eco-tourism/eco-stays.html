Eco-Friendly Stays

Support sustainability by choosing accommodation that cares for the planet.

What to Look For

An eco-friendly stay goes beyond just reusing towels. They actively work to reduce their environmental impact. Look for places that feature:

  • Renewable energy sources
  • Water conservation systems
  • Effective waste management
  • Locally sourced food
  • Community engagement
  • Elimination of single-use plastics

Featured Stays

Netarhat Nature Retreat

Nestled in the serene hills of Netarhat, this retreat is powered entirely by solar energy and practices rainwater harvesting.
