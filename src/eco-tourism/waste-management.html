Smart Waste Management

Working together to keep Jharkhand's beautiful destinations pristine.

Our Initiatives

We are implementing a multi-pronged approach to waste management at popular tourist sites to minimize environmental impact and maintain natural beauty.

Segregated Bins

Color-coded bins for wet, dry, and hazardous waste are placed at key locations.

Reducing Plastics

Partnering with local vendors to promote alternatives to single-use plastics.

Community Composting

Organic waste from eateries is collected and converted into compost for local use.

How You Can Help

  • Carry a reusable water bottle and coffee cup.
  • Properly dispose of your waste in the correct segregated bins.
  • Avoid littering and leave natural sites cleaner than you found them.
  • Support local shops that use sustainable packaging.
